## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

**Script won't execute**
Ensure your executor is fully attached to the Roblox process before running. Restart both applications and try again. 

**GUI doesn't appear after execution**
Press `RightShift` to toggle the dashboard. Some executors introduce a short delay — wait 3–5 seconds before toggling.

**Infinite stamina not working**
This feature occasionally requires a fresh execution after being teleported to a new server. Re-execute the script once you're fully loaded in.

**ESP not showing all players**
ESP syncs with the current server's player list on execution. If players join after you execute, re-run the script to refresh the overlay.

**Auto-hack failing or stopping mid-progress**
Lower the hack speed multiplier in GUI settings. Setting it too high can desync with the server's progress tracking — a value of 1.5–2x is most reliable.

**Troll features not registering on other players**
Some troll actions require proximity to the target. Move closer to the player before activating interaction-based troll options.

**Server hop landing in empty lobbies**
Enable the **minimum player filter** in the server hop settings to only join lobbies with at least 4 players present.
